About Me

I grew up a third culture child, having lived in the Netherlands, Qatar, and my beautiful native land Bosnia and Herzegovina.

My passions are: exploring human culture from an anthropological standpoint, enticing the human mind with provocative art, and my faith.

My Ideas

I have been heavily influenced by my traditional upbringing in South-East Europe, a place where traditional European and Slavic culture are embedded into our interactions with nature. At the same time the Balkans are the only meeting point between Europe and the neighboring oriental cultures, which offer a world of their own.

Cottage Core

The aesthetic of the lifestyle associated to people who lived on the fringes of European villages. It is the primary aesthetic I aim to explore in my artwork.
